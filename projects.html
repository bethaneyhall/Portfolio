E-Collections in Alma & Primo


E-Collections in Alma & Primo was a presentation given during UAB TechTalks. It was meant to inform library workers how electronic resources are managed in Ex Libris Alma and Primo VE for library patrons. Contact me for slides.


E-Resource Usage Statistics During Covid-19


E-Resources Usage Statistics During Covid-19 was presented in front of the Scholarly Support Network at FSU Libraries. The occasion was meant to inform library faculty and staff why usage statistics are important and how they were affected by the COVID-19 pandemic during 2020. Contact me for slides.
