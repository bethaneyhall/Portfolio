"Providing and Maintaining Electronic Resources at an Academic Library" was a presentation I gave on two separate occasions for live audiences in 2022, George A. Smathers Library at University of Florida and Sterne Library at University of Alabama at Birmingham. The presentation is meant to detail the complete electronic resource lifecycle as well as challenges and opportunities associated with providing and maintaining e-resources at an academic library.

UAB Electronic Resources Troubleshooting LibGuide

The Electronic Resources LibGuide is meant to assist library patrons at University of Alabama at Birmingham with locating, accessing, and troubleshooting e-resources.

UAB Electronic Resources News & Updates LibGuide

The Electronic Resources News & Updates LibGuide is meant to showcase new electronic resources acquired by UAB Libraries.

FSU E-Resources During COVID-19


The original presentation was presented live for the FSU Strozier Library Scholarly Support Network on April 15, 2021. The purpose for the event was to share with faculty and staff how usage reports are generated for e-resources, why they are important to interpret, what they mean for collections, and how usage has been affected by the recent COVID-19 pandemic.

The above slideshow consists of SpringShare LibGuides I've published for FSU Libraries.
