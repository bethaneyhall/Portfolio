The above slideshow consists of screenshots of a few SpringShare LibGuides that I've published for FSU Libraries. The first libguide, Temporary Access to Electronic Resources During COVID-19, was made in order to create a centralized place for free and open resources that were offered specifically for the COVID-19 pandemic from 2020-2021. The second libguide, Electronic Resources Troubleshooting, is meant to assist patrons with their electronic resource access questions and concerns. The Google Scholar libguide is meant to assist patrons in using Google Scholar as a research tool.

The proposed redesign of the FSU School of Information website was a group project which consisted of many steps. Personas and scenerios were created first to judge the site's key audience members, following a content audit and content taxonomy report of the current site's information architecture. Sitemaps and wireframes were then developed to showcase the redesign of specific pages of the site. A final project presentation and project proposal were also created that details an evaluation of the redesign process. The following slideshow consists of only the wireframes made. For access to the other documentation from this project, please contact me directly.
