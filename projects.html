The guides I created while working at FSU Libraries are the following (according to order of slideshow): "Temporary Access to E-Resources During COVID-19", "Google Scholar Guide", and "FSU Foundating Fund Resources". To see entire guide(s), contact me.


"Temporary Access to E-Resources During Covid-19" consisted of a list of freely available online resources during the Covid-19 pandemic. These resources were temporarily free for patrons due to the lockdown that took place during the pandemic. The libguide was taken down once the resources were no longer free and the pandemic lockdown ended.


"Google Scholar Libguide" was meant to assist patrons with using Google Scholar as a research tool as well as how to connect GS accounts to the FSU Library catalog.


"FSU Foundation Fund Resources" was a guide that listed the specific electronic collections that were acquired with FSU Foundation Funds.

E-Collections in Alma & Primo


"E-Collections in Alma & Primo" is a presentation given during UAB TechTalks. It was meant to inform library workers how electronic resources are managed in Ex Libris Alma and Primo VE for library patrons. Contact me for slides.


E-Resource Usage Statistics During Covid-19


"E-Resources Usage Statistics During Covid-19" was presented in front of the Scholarly Support Network at FSU Libraries. The occasion was meant to inform library faculty and staff why usage statistics are important and how they were affected by the COVID-19 pandemic during 2020. Contact me for slides.
