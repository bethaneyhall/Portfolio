App Configuration & Implementation



In order to provide Single Sign-On (SSO) through the app for authentication of users, a SAML connection was configured. To ensure patron's can use the app's features, like checking their library account and search the catalog, the app must be able to communicate with Ex Libris Alma and Primo VE. After this connection was established, SMS alerts and notifications were enabled for app users.


Usability Testing


Before the Library Mobile app was distributed and promoted to the public, internal usability testing was done in order to test ease of use of the app and to identify any areas of improvement. Participants were given access to the app then asked to follow prompts. Once testing was completed, participants rated the app from 1 - 5 stars then asked to detail their experience. Changes were made to the app once feedback was collected.


Marketing


Marketing for the app was done by placing flyers with QR codes around the multiple physical library locations as well as digital signage spots. The app was also featured in The UAB Reporter, a newsletter for the entire UAB community, as well as featured on the UAB homepage.
