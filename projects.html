The above slideshow consists of SpringShare LibGuides I've published for FSU Libraries. "Electronic Resources Troubleshooting" is meant to assist patrons with their e-resource access questions and concerns. "Google Scholar" is an instructional material meant to assist patrons in using Google Scholar as a research tool. The final guide features electronic resource collections that were acquired using Foundation Fund donations.

The proposed redesign of the FSU School of Information website was a group project which consisted of many steps. Personas and scenerios were created to judge the site's key audience members, following a content audit and content taxonomy report of the current site's information architecture. Sitemaps and wireframes were then developed to showcase the redesign pages of the site. A final presentation and project proposal were also created. The following slideshow consists of only the wireframes.
